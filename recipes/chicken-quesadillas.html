Chicken Quesadillas

Ingredients

  • Flour tortillas (4)

  • Cooked chicken breast (1 cup, shredded)
  • Shredded cheese (1 cup, cheddar or Mexican blend)

  • Salsa (optional)

  • Sour cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat.

  2. Place one tortilla in the skillet and sprinkle half of the shredded cheese over it.

  3. Add shredded chicken evenly over the cheese.

  4. Top with the remaining cheese and place another tortilla on top.

  5. Cook until the bottom tortilla is golden brown, then carefully flip and cook the other side.

  6. Remove from skillet, cut into wedges, and serve with salsa and sour cream.
