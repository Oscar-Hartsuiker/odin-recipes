Spaghetti Aglio e Oliio

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti (200g)

  • Olive oil (4 tablespoons)

  • Garlic (4 cloves, thinly sliced)

  • Red pepper flakes (1 teaspoon)

  • Parsley (a handful, chopped)

  • Parmesan cheese (optional)

  • Salt

    • Instructions

    1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water according to the package instructions.

    2. While the pasta is cooking, heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.

    3. Add the sliced garlic and red pepper flakes, cooking until the garlic is golden brown.

    4. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the pan. Toss to coat the pasta with the garlic and oil.

    5. Add chopped parsley and mix well. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
